About INNOVISION'18

INNOVISION, a platform celebrating every vision that creates innovations. Staged first in the year 2004, Innovision now stands as the most spectacular techno-management fest of the region. Rising to a higher stratum of science every year, the fest has chronicled its glory all over the country. Events, workshops and exhibitions hosting a convergence of intelligence and technology have witnessed the footfall of thousands of young minds. Innovision 2018 is all set to raise the curtain, and this time a new sci-fi Odyssey will beat the drum.

WHAT FACTORS DRIVE INNOVISION 2018?

Science Fiction

INNOVISION 2018, one of the largest techno-management fests of India, is on an odyssey to stretch out one's imaginations to the far reaches, and extract the technology that one sees in their mind's eye.

Out-of-box

Team INNOVISION aims at breaking down boundaries and thinking out of the box. After all, science fiction is all about letting your imagination run wild and capturing them into spectacular inventions.

Social Responsibility

With the world moving at a breakneck pace, it is important to ensure that everyone moves together. Team INNOVISION recognizes the need to uphold our social values and virtues to achieve true development.
